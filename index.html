About Us

Sri Abirami Women Hostel is designed to provide a safe and comfortable living space for women in Theni. The hostel offers clean rooms, hygienic meals, and a peaceful environment suitable for students and working professionals.

We understand the importance of a home away from home. Our friendly management ensures that all your needs are met, providing an affordable monthly rent without compromising on quality and security.
